1. Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 100g pancetta
  • Black pepper and salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  4. Mix the spaghetti with the pancetta, then add the egg mixture off the heat.
  5. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan and black pepper.
